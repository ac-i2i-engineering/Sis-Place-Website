Sis' Place Inc.

support for all our sisters

Proudly supporting survivors since 2024.

We serve the community of Springfield MA and surrounding areas by standing with survivors of domestic violence. Our approach focuses first on addressing urgent housing, health, and safety needs. This allows for stability and empowers each individual to build a life they choose.

we provide

safe housing

Our shelter provides transitional housing for women and children in need. We welcome you as you are.

mental health support

Individual and group mental health support options are available for participants and community members. We honor your experience.

community living

Residents of the shelter live in community with other survivors. We lean on each other.

peer advocacy

Staff and volunteers provide support for all your next steps: housing, legal tasks, healthcare, and more. We've been there and we care.

our mission

We help build a future that belongs to everyone - free of domestic violence. By meeting survivors' immediate needs and offering personalized support, we show up without judgment or conditions. Every survivor's journey is unique, and we pledge to support each individual on their own terms.

contact us

Navigating something tough? Want to learn more about Sis' Place or get involved? Reach out - we're here.
